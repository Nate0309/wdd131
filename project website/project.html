Site Name:

Adeptus Finder

This name is a play on the terminology used within the Warhammer universe, and relates the information that one can find places to play Warhammer

Purpose:

To help newcomers to the Warhammer franchise find clubs, places, or friends to play with, and to help them start building their army to take to the nearest location to play.

Scenarios:

  • What faction should I use as a beginner to build my army
  • Where is the closest store or club I can go to to participate

Color Schema

Heading backgrounds will be: #0D407F, Macragge Blue

Background for other sections will be: #991115 Mephiston Red

The general background color will be Astroganite #787878

Typography:

Text will all be in the bungee format
